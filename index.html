About Me

Welcome! My names Amelia and I am a junior at Michigan State University. I am currently in the College of Communication Arts and Sciences studying Creative Advertising. Throughout my life I have always had a passion and a knack for drawing and painting. I enjoy designing visuals that spark curiousity, and am excited to be able to integrate my passions into my daily work. In my free time I love being outdoors. I find great enjoyment in running as well as hiking. I have lived in Michigan my entire life, growing up in Kalamazoo, and going to school at MSU. Another pastime I have, weather permitting, is skiing. I think the reason I love doing these things so much is because each of them helps me clear my mind to allow for new ideas to flow in. There is no better therapy than the outdoors.
